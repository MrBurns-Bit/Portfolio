About

Precision work,
built to last.

I'm Austin Burns, a finish carpenter and custom millwork contractor based in Miami Beach with deep roots in the U.S. Virgin Islands. My background spans post-and-beam construction, hospitality buildouts, and full residential renovation — which means I'm comfortable moving between fine detail work and full project management.

I specialize in custom cabinetry, architectural millwork, and built-ins for clients who care about craftsmanship as much as function. Every project gets the same standard: clean joinery, durable finish work, and a build that's made to last.
